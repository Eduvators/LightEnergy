Light Energy Transformation

In electrical circuits, loads consume electrical energy and convert it into various forms of energy. One important transformation is the conversion to light energy.

Light Energy

When electrical energy flows through certain components, it can be transformed into light energy, illuminating surroundings.

Example: In a light bulb, electric current passing through the filament generates heat, which then transforms into visible light energy. This principle is used in vehicle headlights, dashboard indicators, and interior lighting.
